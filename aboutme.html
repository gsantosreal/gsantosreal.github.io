A bit about... me!

My name is Gael Camil Santos Rodriguez. I am a sophomore computer science major and Spanish minor from New York by way of the Dominican Republic. My primary passions are for computer science, language learning, and activism.

I first found passion in computer science in 5th grade, through SCRATCH and also my love for video games. I have since taken computer science classes at every opportunity, and I am majoring in it now in college.

I've been interested in learning languages ever since I could. Since I am Hispanic, Spanish has always been a language of interest for me. I've dabbled a few languages over the years, such as Chinese, French, and even a bit of Swahili.

Activism has always been an interest of mine due to my upbringing. Though I was raised in the Bronx, full of minority cultures and social awareness, I moved to suburban New York before 10th grade, which was a complete reversal from what I was used to. I had experienced systemic discrimination with my own five senses, and I can't stand for it.

P.S.: As of right now, I have no intention of cutting my hair.
(Hopefully it stays that way!)
