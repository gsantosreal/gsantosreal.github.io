GAEL SANTOS:

Programmer - Activist - Language Enthusiast

My name is Gael Camil Santos Rodriguez. I am a Dominican-American high school student at Tappan Zee High School. My primary passions are for Computer Science, Psychology, and Activism.

On my free time I enjoy hanging out / talking with friends, playing video games, coding, watching history videos on YouTube, and learning languages. I am currently learning Mandarin Chinese and intend to learn Swahili and French.
